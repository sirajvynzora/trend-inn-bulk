Innovation That Drives Growth

We're proud to have built lasting relationships with businesses.

A platform built to create entrepreneurs and connect businesses across India

Trend in Bulk was created in Kerala when we faced many challenges while trying to build a new store. One of the biggest problems was finding reliable vendors and suppliers. Most wholesale suppliers were located in the north side of India, which made sourcing products difficult for businesses in the south. This challenge inspired us to build a platform that connects businesses across the country.

Our Story

Started in Kerala with a mission to solve the sourcing problem faced by south Indian entrepreneurs.

Our Network

We bridge North and South India, creating a supply network where products can move across regions easily.
